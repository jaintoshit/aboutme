Hi!

I'm a Research Associate working with Prof. Vijay Natarajan's group at the Indian Institute of Science.

My research at the Visualization and Graphics Lab focusses on coming up with interactive and scalable visualizations for analysing and exploring high resolution ocean data. I focus on designing and implementing a visualization, bringing together concepts from parallel processing, computational topology and geometry.
Prior to this, I had a stint at Walmart Global Tech as a software engineer.

I did my undergraduate thesis under Prof. Tathagata Ray and Prof. Sandeep S. Deshmukh, where I worked on creating a machine vision based system that sorts various assembly parts of an aerospace vehicle for the Defence Research and Development Organisation, Government of India. I graduated with a B.E. in Computer Science from BITS Pilani, Hyderabad, India, in 2021.
